Use this prompt with your design tool (Figma, Canva, Adobe Express, Midjourney, etc.) to generate all remaining assets for Play Store and App Store submission.

## Brand Reference

**App Name:** Family Bible
**Tagline ideas:** "Scripture for the whole family" / "Read together. Grow together."
**Icon:** Open book with lined pages, gold accent bar beneath - warm rounded-square icon
**Typography:** Lora (serif, for headings/display) + Source Serif 4 (serif, for body)
**Design Language:** Warm, earthy, premium - NOT generic church/religious blue

## ASSET 6: App Store Metadata Text

### Short Description (Play Store, max 80 chars):
```
Read the Bible together as a family. 7 translations, offline, beautifully simple.
```

### Subtitle (App Store, max 30 chars):
```
Family Scripture Reading
```

### Keywords (App Store, max 100 chars):
```
bible,scripture,family,reading,kjv,asv,offline,verse,search,bookmark,christian,faith,devotional
```

### Full Description (both stores, max 4000 chars):
```
Family Bible brings the Word of God to your family in a beautiful, distraction-free reading experience.

WHY FAMILY BIBLE?

Unlike other Bible apps cluttered with ads and social features, Family Bible focuses on what matters: reading Scripture together. Clean typography, warm design, and powerful features — all without the noise.

FEATURES

- 7 Trusted Translations: Read in ASV, KJV, BBE, DBY, WBT, WEB, and YLT
- Family Sharing: Invite family members to read together and share annotations
- Powerful Search: Find any verse instantly across the entire Bible
- Bookmarks & Annotations: Save, highlight, and annotate your favorite passages
- Offline Reading: The complete Bible is stored on your device — no internet required
- Beautiful Typography: Carefully chosen serif fonts (Lora & Source Serif 4) designed for long reading
- Dark Mode: Comfortable reading day or night
- Verse Sharing: Share beautiful verse cards with friends and family
- Chapter Navigation: Jump to any book, chapter, and verse with ease
- Daily Notifications: Optional reminders to stay in the Word

BUILT FOR FAMILIES

Family Bible isn't just another Bible app. It's designed for households who want to grow in faith together. Share bookmarks, discuss annotations, and keep everyone on the same page — literally.

PRIVACY FIRST

No ads. No tracking. No account required for solo reading. Your reading life is yours.

Download Family Bible today and experience Scripture the way it was meant to be read — together.
```

## Design Tool Export Checklist

When exporting, ensure:
- [ ] All PNGs are flattened (no alpha on icons)
- [ ] Screenshots have no actual device bezels bleeding off-edge
- [ ] Text is legible at small sizes (store listing thumbnails)
- [ ] No placeholder or "Lorem ipsum" text visible in screenshots
- [ ] Feature graphic looks good cropped to center (Google sometimes crops edges)
- [ ] All files named clearly: `screenshot_01_reading.png`, `feature_graphic_dark.png`, etc.
